## mybatis 动态sql

```sql
<select id="findActiveBlogWithTitleLike" parameterType="Blog" resultType="Blog">
  SELECT * FROM BLOG WHERE state = ‘ACTIVE’
  <if test="title != null and title != ''">
    AND title like #{title}
  </if>
</select>
```
